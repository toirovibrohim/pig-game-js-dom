How To Play?

- First you need to enter the players names. And set the winning score. After that roll the dice alternately.

- Player wins by being the first player to score N-number (which he/she will enter manually) or more points.

Game rules

- If the player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points he accumulated that turn, and he passes the turn to the next player.

- If the player rolls a 1 six times, his total point will be reset and passes the turn to the next player. Play passes from player to player until a winner is determined.

- Indicator of ones will be shown each side bottom. Every time player rolls one it will increase. Once reaches six it will reset global score and itself also.

- First player that reaches N-number wins the game.
